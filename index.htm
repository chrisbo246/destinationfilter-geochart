Getting started

  1. Select your actual country to pre fill your profile. Note that your previous profile will be replaced.

  2. Go to "My Profile" tab and verify that the values ​​are correct.
  3. Go to "My priorities" tab and reorder values depending on your priorities.
  4. Go to "Map" tab and click "refresh map" button to view the best destinations.

Map control

  • Click an area to show details.
  • Double-click area to zoom in.
  • Right-click map and select "return" to zoom out.
